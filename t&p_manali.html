Manali @ 5,250/-* Per Person

Highlights

Manali is a popular hill station located in the Kullu Valley in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is a popular tourist destination, renowned for its scenic beauty, charming old-world charm and adventurous opportunities. Manali offers breathtaking panoramic views of the mountains and is surrounded by lush green forests and apple orchards.

Manali is a hub for adventure sports such as river rafting, paragliding, and trekking. It is also known for its hot springs and the famous Vashisht Temple, dedicated to Lord Rama's sage, Vashisht. The temple is known for its hot springs and is believed to have healing properties. Tourists can also visit the Hadimba Temple, which is an ancient wooden temple dedicated to the demon-goddess, Hadimba.

Overall, Manali is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a break from the city life, surrounded by stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Whether you are an adventure enthusiast, nature lover, or simply looking for a peaceful holiday, Manali has something to offer for everyone.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi to Manali (530 Kms / 12-14 Hrs)

  • At 5:00 PM, you are required to report at the Delhi Volvo pickup point on your own and then depart for Manali.
  • The Delhi Manali packages offer a unique experience for everyone, be it peace for meditators, inspiration for artists, daring activities for adventure seekers, and more.
  • The journey to Manali is characterized by cool breezes that take away all your stress, calming you with serene surroundings, and leaving you in awe of its jaw-dropping landscapes.

Day 2: Arrival in Manali and Local City Tour (3-4 Hrs)

  • You will reach Manali in the morning and be transferred to the hotel.
  • After freshening up, proceed for a local sightseeing tour of Manali, including visits to the Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, and the Tibetan Monastery.
  • In the evening, you will have free time to explore the Mall Road and IBEX Market of Manali. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: MANALI - SOLANG VALLEY / ROHTANG PASS (52 KMS/ 3 HRS) - MANALI

  • after breakfast, enjoy a tour to Solang Valley where you can marvel at the beautiful surroundings and participate in various exciting adventure activities at your own expense.
  • Alternatively, visit the stunning Rohtang Pass, located 13,000 feet above sea level on the Manali-Leh highway. Discover the breathtaking scenery, snow, and famous attractions like Khoti, Gulaba, Mahri, Rahni Nala, and the source of the Beas River - Beas Kund.
  • Please note that Rohtang Pass is subject to permit availability and is closed every Tuesday. Vehicles are limited to a certain number daily by the National Green Tribunal. Permits may be acquired on the spot at an additional cost on a sharing or individual basis, depending on the guest's preference.
  • After a day of exploration, return to Manali for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Manali to Delhi (530 Kms / 12-14 Hrs)

  • you will check out of the hotel at 10:00 AM and have time to explore the local market, Van Vihar (a dense forest with Deodar trees), and the Tibetan Monastery in the market on your own.
  • At 4:30 PM, you are required to report at the Manali Private Delivery Stand and return to Delhi.
