Highlights

Shimla, located at an altitude of 2200m, is the capital and largest city of Himachal Pradesh in India. Surrounded by breathtaking hills and dense forests, Shimla has been a sought-after destination for families and newlyweds for the past 50 years.

The British were so fond of the city that they made it their summer capital in 1864 and used to govern the subcontinent from here during the hot summer months. Today, Shimla still holds onto its vintage charm with charming colonial architecture, a pedestrian-friendly Mall Road and lovely churches.

Tourists visit Shimla throughout the year, with the weather being particularly pleasant during the summer months. Winters are cold, with occasional snowfall from mid-December to the end of February. Visitors flock to the centrally located Mall Road and the Ridge, a pedestrian-only area that features numerous shops, cafes and restaurants.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi to Shimla by Volvo (370 km, 9-10 hours)

  • Depart from Delhi's ISBT Kashmiri gate/Majnu ka Tilla in the evening at 9:00 pm for a 9-10 hour journey to Shimla, the "Queen of Hills".
  • The distance between Delhi and Shimla is approximately 370 km and the journey will end at the Shimla bus stand.

Day 2: Arrival in Shimla [Local Sightseeing]

  • On arrival in Shimla, you will be picked up from the bus stand and transferred to your pre-booked hotel.
  • After freshening up, you will start your first day of sightseeing by visiting Jakhu Temple and Advance Study.
  • In the evening, you will visit The Mall, Scandal Point, Ridge, Lakkar Bazaar, and Christ Church. Dinner and overnight stay in the hotel.

Day 3: Excursion to Kufri (22 Kms/45 mins)

  • On the third day, you will have an excursion to Kufri, which is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular picnic spot in the summer months.
  • After visiting Kufri, you will return to Shimla and continue exploring The Mall, Scandal Point, Ridge, Lakkar Bazaar, and Christ Church. Overnight stay in the hotel.

Day 4: Shimla to Delhi (370 Kms/8-10 hrs)

  • On the final day, you will check out from the hotel in the morning and drive to the Shimla Volvo stand for your return trip to Delhi.
