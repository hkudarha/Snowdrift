Manali Shimla By Volvo @ 8,000/-* Per Person

Highlights

Shimla and Manali are two of the most popular hill stations in India, known for their scenic beauty and tranquil atmosphere. Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and is situated in the northern part of India. It is surrounded by snow-capped peaks and dense forests, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. The town is famous for its colonial architecture, including the Viceregal Lodge, the Christ Church, and the Gaiety Theatre, which are now preserved as tourist attractions.

Manali, on the other hand, is a small hill station located in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh. It is known for its scenic beauty, adventure activities, and natural hot springs. The town is surrounded by towering peaks, rolling hills, and lush greenery, making it an ideal place for trekking and camping. Manali is also a popular destination for skiing and snowboarding, with several ski resorts located in the area. The town is known for its vibrant culture and tradition, with local festivals and fairs being held throughout the year.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi to Manali (530 Kms/12-14 hrs)

  • Report at Delhi Volvo pick up point at 5:00 pm to start your Himachal Pradesh trip.
  • Take an overnight journey to Manali by Volvo.
  • Manali offers something special for everyone - peace for meditators, inspiration for artists, and daring activities for adventure seekers.
  • On the way to Manali, experience the cool breeze and the serene environment, while being surprised by the breathtaking landscapes.

Day 2: Arrival in Manali and Local City Tour

  • Arrive at Manali Volvo Stand and be welcomed by a representative.
  • Transfer to the hotel (check-in time is 12:00 PM, early check-in subject to availability).
  • After freshening up, proceed for a local sightseeing tour of Manali.
  • Visit Hadimba Devi Temple (dedicated to Himachal's significant deity) and Vashisht Hot Springs (well-known for its hot curable water).
  • Spend the evening exploring the Mall Road and IBEX Market.
  • Overnight stay in the hotel.

Day 3: MANALI - SOLANG VALLEY / ROHTANG PASS (52 KMS/ 3 HRS) - MANALI

  • after breakfast, enjoy a tour to Solang Valley where you can marvel at the beautiful surroundings and participate in various exciting adventure activities at your own expense.
  • Alternatively, visit the stunning Rohtang Pass, located 13,000 feet above sea level on the Manali-Leh highway. Discover the breathtaking scenery, snow, and famous attractions like Khoti, Gulaba, Mahri, Rahni Nala, and the source of the Beas River - Beas Kund.
  • Please note that Rohtang Pass is subject to permit availability and is closed every Tuesday. Vehicles are limited to a certain number daily by the National Green Tribunal. Permits may be acquired on the spot at an additional cost on a sharing or individual basis, depending on the guest's preference.
  • After a day of exploration, return to Manali for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: MANALI TO SHIMLA VIA KULLU (260KMS / 8 HRS)

  • After breakfast in the morning, check out from your hotel in Manali and proceed to the Queen of Hills, Shimla, by deluxe bus on a sharing basis.
  • Upon reaching Shimla, check into your hotel and spend the night there.

Day 5 : SHIMLA SIGHTSEEING -> DELHI (370 KMS/8-10 HRS)

  • Start the day with a fresh mind and spirit, after breakfast proceed for a sightseeing tour of Shimla.
  • Visit popular tourist spots like Kufri, known for its ski slopes in the winter and a popular picnic spot in the summer, Jakhu Temple, and Vice regal Lodge.
  • n the evening, explore the bustling streets of The Mall, Scandal Point, Ridge, Lakkar Bazaar, and admire the beauty of Christ Church.
  • After a day full of sightseeing and exploration, have your dinner after that at 8:00 pm, report at Shimla Volvo stand for your return trip to Delhi, with memories that will last a lifetime.
