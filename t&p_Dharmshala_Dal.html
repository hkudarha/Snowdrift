Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi to Dharamshala (480 km, 11-13 hours overnight journey)

  • Depart from Delhi at 8:00 pm and embark on an overnight journey to Dharamshala.
  • Take an overnight journey to Dharamshala by Volvo.
  • the city offers stunning waterfalls, tea gardens, a cricket stadium, and a peaceful environment due to the presence of the Dalai Lama.

Day 2: Dharamshala Arrival and Local Sightseeing

  • Upon arrival, our representative will greet you and transfer you to the hotel.
  • After freshening up, explore the official residence of the Dalai Lama, McLeod Ganj, Bhagsunath Temple, and Dal Lake.
  • In the evening, you are free to roam around the local market and Kotwai Bazaar.

Day 3: Kangra Valley Excursion from Dharamshala

  • After breakfast, head to Kangra Valley to visit the Brajeshwari Devi Temple and Kangra Fort.
  • After sightseeing, return to Dharamshala for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Dharamshala to Dalhousie (145 km, 5-6 hours)

  • Check out from the hotel in Dharamshala and drive to the next hill station, Dalhousie.
  • Dalhousie, known for its scenic beauty. Upon arrival, check into the hotel and enjoy the evening exploring the local market.

Day 5: Dalhousie to Khajjiar (22 km, 45 minutes)

  • After breakfast, proceed to the picturesque Khajjiar, known as the mini Switzerland of India.
  • Admire the mesmerizing beauty of the pine forests and lake, and participate in activities like zorbing and trekking.
  • After this wonderful experience, return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 6: Dalhousie to Delhi via Dharamshala (625 km, 16-17 hours)

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Dharamshala.
  • You will then be dropped off at the Dharamshala delivery stand for your return trip to Delhi.
