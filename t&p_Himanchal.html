Himachal Harmony @ 22,550/-* Per Person

Highlights

The majestic snow-capped peaks and picturesque river valleys of Himachal Pradesh make it a premier destination for outdoor adventures. From trekking and mountaineering to rafting, paragliding, and skiing, this region has something for everyone who loves mountain sports. The diverse topography of interlocking mountain chains also makes Himachal a wonderful place to explore, whether by bus, car, motorcycle, jeep, or on foot. Each pass that you cross into a new valley presents you with a different world, complete with its own unique culture, gods, and even language.

Villages nestled on steep slopes captivate visitors with their fairy-tale architecture and the warmth of their people. The hill stations offer a holiday atmosphere and colonial charm, while backpacker destinations draw in travelers with their laid-back vibes and stunning mountain views. In McLeod Ganj, the home-away-from-home of the Dalai Lama, or in the remote Lahaul and Spiti regions with their centuries-old Buddhist cultures, you may feel as though you've stumbled into Tibet.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi and transfer to Shimla (370 KMS/10-11 HRS)

  • You will arrive in Delhi either at the airport or railway station and catch a cab to Shimla. Upon arrival in Shimla, you will check into the hotel and have dinner.
  • The journey from Delhi to Shimla covers a distance of 370 kilometers and takes approximately 10-11 hours by car.
  • Shimla is renowned as the "Queen of Hills" due to its captivating landscapes and colonial heritage, which has maintained its allure from the time of British India till present day.

Day 2: Sightseeing in Shimla and Kufri

  • After breakfast, you will go on a trip to Kufri which is located 20 km from Shimla.
  • Start the day with a visit to the Kufri Zoo and have fun at the park with outdoor or indoor games, Indulge in horse riding or skiing (in winter).
  • After that, explore the Jakhu Temple and advance study.
  • In the evening, stroll through the famous Mall, Scandal Point, Ridge, Lakkar Bazaar, and Christ Church before retiring for the night at the hotel.
  • Overnight stay in the hotel.

Day 3: Drive to Manali (260 KMs/7-8 hours)

  • Start your day with a delicious breakfast After breakfast and check out from the hotel, you will proceed to Manali.
  • On the way, you will visit Sunder Nagar Lake, Pandoh Dam, Hanogi Mata Temple, Kullu, and Vaishno Devi Temple.
  • pon arrival in Manali, you will check into the hotel and have dinner.

Day 4: Local city tour of Manali

  • After breakfast, you will go on a local city tour of Manali, visiting places like Hadimba devi temple, Vishisht Rishi, Club House, mall road, IBEX market, Tibetan monastery, and nature park.
  • After the tour, you will return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Excursion to Solang Valley or Rohtang Pass

  • Begin your day with a delicious breakfast and embark on an exciting journey to either Solang Valley or Rohtang Pass.
  • Solang Valley, known for its stunning views and thrilling adventure activities, transforms into a winter wonderland with a blanket of snow as winter sets in.Indulge in various activities at your own cost before returning to the hotel for an overnight stay.
  • Alternatively, visit the stunning Rohtang Pass, located 13,000 feet above sea level on the Manali-Leh highway. Discover the breathtaking scenery, snow, and famous attractions like Khoti, Gulaba, Mahri, Rahni Nala, and the source of the Beas River - Beas Kund.
  • Please note that Rohtang Pass is subject to permit availability and is closed every Tuesday. Vehicles are limited to a certain number daily by the National Green Tribunal. Permits may be acquired on the spot at an additional cost on a sharing or individual basis, depending on the guest's preference.
  • After a day of exploration, return to Manali for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Drive to DHARAMSHALA (240 KMS / 7-8 HRS)

  • After breakfast, bid farewell to Manali and embark on a scenic drive to Dharamshala, a haven for Tibetan and Buddhist culture situated in the western region of Himachal Pradesh.
  • The drive is expected to take about 7-8 hours. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 7: Sightseeing in Dharamshala

  • Start your day with a delicious breakfast, then set out to explore the vibrant city of Dharamshala, located just 3 km away.
  • During the day, you will visit the captivating Bhagsunath Temple, the stunning Bhugsu Waterfall, the peaceful residence of the Dalai Lama, the charming Mcleodganj, and the iconic Dharamshala Cricket Stadium.
  • After a full day of exploring, retire to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 8: Drive to Dalhousie (145 KMS/5-6 HRS)

  • After breakfast, you will drive to Dalhousie, also known as the mini Switzerland of India due to its scenic beauty.
  • The drive is expected to take about 5-6 hours. Upon arrival, you will check into the hotel and have an overnight stay.

Day 9: DALHOUSIE to KHAJJIAR (22 KM/45 MINS)

  • After breakfast, you will go on an excursion to Khajjiar, where you can admire the beauty of the Khajjair Lake, wander in the 9-hole golf course, or just relax and feel at peace.
  • After the excursion, you will return to the hotel for overnight stay in Dalhousie.

Day 10: DALHOUSIE to DELHI (555 KMS / 14-16 HRS)

  • After breakfast, you will check out from the hotel and return to Delhi, while recalling all the memorable moments of your trip.
