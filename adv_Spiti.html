Spiti Bike Tour

Highlights

If you're an adventurous motorcyclist, a trip to Spiti in the Himalayan region of India might be just what you need. The journey starts with a stop at the world's highest petrol pump in Kaza, where you can fuel up and prepare for an exhilarating ride through the valley's changing landscapes, tricky terrain, and awe-inspiring high altitude views.

During the expedition, you'll come across some impressive sights, including Asia's highest suspended bridge, the Chicham Bridge. As you ride over it, you'll feel like you're on top of the world, looking down at a tiny truck below. After a long journey, you'll have a chance to rest at the Key monastery, which is over 1,000 years old and sits in a rugged, serene setting.

Don't forget to capture your conquest of this stunning location with a photo by one of the sparkling turquoise lakes. It'll be a memento of a once-in-a-lifetime adventure that you'll cherish forever.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Depart from Delhi to Manal

  • Once you reach Delhi, you will have to proceed towards your boarding point. You will then be taking an AC Volvo overnight bus leaving for Manali.

Day 2: Manali

  • On the first day, you will be reaching Manali and check-in at Hotel. Rest and spend some time witnessing the beauty of nature and get ready for the orientation process and In the afternoon met our bike expert. He will assist you about the bikes and routes.
  • Take a city tour by motor bike and get familiar with the bikes. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3: Manali to Jibhi

  • Early in the morning, meet your road captain who will be leading the entire trip and will brief you about the terrain and will draw out a day-wise final plan. After the briefing, you will be introduced with your motorcycles that you will be riding for the rest of the tour.
  • Start your ride by taking the Manali - Shimla Highway, and after crossing the Aut Tunnel take a left turn to reach Jibhi.
  • On reaching Jibhi, check in to the hotel and take a rest for a while.
  • Located in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, Jibhi is an alluring village surrounded by natural beauty and gives you the taste of an offbeat destination. As you reach Jibhi, the favourite backpacker destination, you can try out trout fishing, and the Great Himalayan National park. Overnight stay at your hotel in Jibhi.

Day 4: Jibhi to Rampur

  • Wake up to a beautiful sunrise in Jibhi and have a delicious breakfast. You will then be travelling through the rich forests of Deodar, breathtaking views of the valley, snow covered mountain peaks and fast flowing rivers.
  • Once you reach Rampur you will check in to the hotel and take some rest. Later in the evening, explore the surroundings of the hotel. Overnight stay in Rampur

Day 5: Rampur to Chitkul

  • Wake up early in the morning to experience one of the most beautiful sunrises. After a delicious breakfast you will leave for one of the most beautiful views of Kinnaur Valley. Travel along the Sutlej river till Karcham from where you will take a right turn towards Chitkul following the Baspa river.
  • Chitkul is the last inhabited village before the Indo-China border and here you can also visit the famous ‘Hindustan ka Akhri Dhaba’ for an amazing meal. Overnight stay and dinner at hotel/homestay in Chitkul.

Day 6: Chitkul to Kalpa

  • After having a hearty breakfast, you will leave for Kalpa.
  • You will be driving back towards karcham from where you will proceed deeper into the valley towards Powari. At Powari take a left to ride to ReoKong Peo, which is the main town of Kinnaur district from where you will proceed to Kalpa.
  • Spend the night in the beautiful Kalpa village overlooking Kinnaur Kailash mountains. Overnight stay at camp/homestay in Kalpa.

Day 7: Kalpa to Kaza

  • After an early morning breakfast, you will be leaving for Kaza passing through the beautiful Spiti Valley.
  • En-route, you will be taking the Indo-Tiber Highway and pass through Khab bridge that marks the confluence of Spiti and the Satluj River.
  • Take a short stopover at Nako Lake and have lunch, after which you will be entering the Spiti Valley right as soon as you cross Sumdo.
  • Just 3 Km after Sumdo, take a detour on the right through the gate to visit the 500-Year-Old Mummy in the Gue Village.
  • Overnight stay in Kaza.

Day 8: Sightseeing in Kaza

  • Post early morning breakfast, take your bike out today to explore the spectacular beauty of Kaza.
  • Visit the tourist spots including the Key Gompa Monastery that is nestled between the mountains in a bowl-shaped valley of the small village Langza in Spiti valley.
  • Langza lies at a whopping height of about 14,500 ft. above sea level and also considered to be one of the highest villages in the world with a motorable road and the nearby Komic Village is the highest village.
  • Hikkim is a village in the Spiti district, close to Kaza which is one of the highest year-round inhabited locations in India.
  • After exploring the beautiful villages of Langza, Hikkim and Komik, return back to your hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Kaza.

Day 9: Kaza to Chandratal via Kunzum

  • Post breakfast, your motorbike expedition will be moving towards the next location which is the beautiful crescent-shaped Chandratal Lake.
  • After passing Kunzum La you will take a right before Baltal to visit Chandratal Lake.
  • The beauty of this place is spectacular and you will have to park your vehicles and then trek for two kilometres to reach the lake.
  • The lake is situated in the Spiti part of the Lahaul and Spiti district and is also called the “Moon Lake” due to its crescent shape.
  • It is overlooked by the mountains on one side and a cirque on the other.
  • After the sunset, the lake glitters under the starlit sky adding to its overall charm.
  • Overnight stay at the camps near Chandratal.

Day 10: Chandratal Lake to Manali

  • Today you will be leaving behind the Spiti Valley and start riding towards Manali.
  • After reaching Manali you will check in to the hotel and will be handing over your bikes and this will mark the end of your amazing motorbiking expedition.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 11: Manali to Delhi | Bid Farewell to the Mountains

  • After breakfast, you will be boarding a Volvo AC Bus from Manali to Delhi and will be reaching Delhi by evening.
  • Once you reach Delhi, this marks the end of your memorable tour to Spiti Valley.
