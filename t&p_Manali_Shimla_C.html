Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi to Shimla (370 KM, 10-11 Hours)

  • Arrive at the airport or railway station and get picked up by your cab.
  • Check into the hotel upon arrival and enjoy dinner and an overnight stay.
  • Head towards Shimla, your first destination on this Himachal Pradesh tour.

Day 2: Shimla - Kufri - Shimla (22 KM, 45 Minutes)

  • After breakfast, set out to visit Kufri, located 20 km from Shimla.
  • Explore the zoo, fun park and participate in outdoor activities like horse riding and skiing in the winter season.
  • Visit Jakhu Temple, Advance Study and other famous tourist attractions such as The Mall, Scandal Point, Ridge, Lakkar Bazaar and Christ Church.
  • Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali (260 KM, 7-8 Hours)

  • Start the day with breakfast and check-out from the hotel.
  • Begin your journey to Manali and stop at popular tourist destinations such as Sunder Nagar Lake, Pandoh Dam, Hanogi Mata Temple and Kullu.
  • Arrive in Manali, check into the hotel and enjoy dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Manali Local City Tour (3-4 Hours)

  • After breakfast, embark on a local city tour of Manali.
  • Visit attractions such as Hadimba Devi Temple, Vishisht Rishi, Club House, Mall Road, IBEX market, Tibetan Monastery and Nature Park.
  • Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: MANALI - SOLANG VALLEY / ROHTANG PASS (52 KMS/ 3 HRS) - MANALI

  • After breakfast, enjoy a tour to Solang Valley where you can marvel at the beautiful surroundings and participate in various exciting adventure activities at your own expense.
  • Alternatively, visit the stunning Rohtang Pass, located 13,000 feet above sea level on the Manali-Leh highway. Discover the breathtaking scenery, snow, and famous attractions like Khoti, Gulaba, Mahri, Rahni Nala, and the source of the Beas River - Beas Kund.
  • Please note that Rohtang Pass is subject to permit availability and is closed every Tuesday. Vehicles are limited to a certain number daily by the National Green Tribunal. Permits may be acquired on the spot at an additional cost on a sharing or individual basis, depending on the guest's preference.
  • After a day of exploration, return to Manali for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Manali to Delhi (570 KM, 13-14 Hours)

  • Have breakfast and get ready for your return journey to Delhi.
  • Drop off at the railway station or airport to catch your onward flight or train.
