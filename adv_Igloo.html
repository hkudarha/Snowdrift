Igloo Stay

Experience the thrill of staying in an igloo at Sethan Valley, Manali - a magical destination nestled amidst the snow-capped mountains, providing an ideal retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city. At an altitude of 10,000 feet from sea level, this campsite is marked by breathtaking panoramic views of snow-capped mountains and white sheets of snow all around.The igloo stay experience in Manali is a one-of-a-kind adventure that offers you the chance to spend a night in an igloo, complete with warm beds and comfortable blankets. The igloo stay is perfect for all those who seek comfort in the cold, as well as the thrill of trying something unique.

There are four igloos available, accommodating a minimum of two and a maximum of four people at a time. The igloos come equipped with amenities like common washrooms, comfortable beds, and warm blankets.During your stay, you can witness the mesmerizing sunrise, with the majestic snow-capped mountains as your backdrop. The campsite offers an array of outdoor activities, including skiing, snowboarding, snowman making, and tube slides. You can also enjoy a bonfire with music in the evening with your family and friends.

  • There are 4 Igloo's which offers accommodation to up to min of 2 and a max of 4 people at a time with amenities like common washrooms, beds, comfortable blankets.
  • Witness the mesmerizing sunrise along with the views of the snow-capped mountains from the comfort of your campsite.
  • The campsite offers multiple outdoor activities such as skiing, Snowboarding, snowman making and tube slide.
  • Enjoy a bonfire with music in the evening with your family and friends.
  • The guests can park their vehicles at the appointed parking space provided by the campsite.
  • Snow dress (jumpsuit) and snowshoes will be provided during check-in.
  • Hot water is available for bathing purposes.

How To Reach

Sethan Valley, Manali is situated 13.4 km from Mall Road and can be easily accessed using public or private modes of transportation.

Important Points

  • The igloo site is a 45-minute drive to the mountain top that can be reached using a shared vehicle, the transfers can be availed at an extra cost with the assistance of the camp operator at the base camp.
  • A minimum of 2 people are required to book this package.
  • Children below 5 years of age can stay for free.
